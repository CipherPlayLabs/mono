# CipherPlay Creative Production Kit

Date: 2026-06-03
Status: reusable production guidance

## Purpose

This kit turns the CipherPlay media assets, investor-first brand strategy, and live public site language into a reusable creative system for social posts, short animations, Canva templates, launch cards, and Creative Production prompts.

It is designed for investor-first brand work where public evidence, claim discipline, and technical credibility matter more than visual spectacle.

## Core Creative Thesis

CipherPlay should feel like a research-backed emerging-technology studio that makes noisy markets easier to diligence.

The visual system should say:

- We publish what we see.
- We build from research into software proof.
- We keep claims bounded by observable facts.
- We give investors, partners, and customers a clear next step.

The creative should not say:

- Generic software agency.
- Crypto hype project.
- Research blog with decorative visuals.
- Personal brand.
- Loose collection of unrelated experiments.

## Brand Spine

Primary audience:

- Investors evaluating CipherPlay's company model, research posture, product proof, team, backers, partner ecosystem, and private-material request path.

Supporting audiences:

- Partners evaluating collaboration and ecosystem leverage.
- Customers evaluating research-backed architecture, prototypes, integrations, RANDAO demos, or consulting discovery.

Master promise:

```text
Research-backed software venture formation for emerging-technology markets.
```

Plain public description:

```text
CipherPlay is an emerging-technology software studio and market research firm connecting research, product thesis development, and software execution.
```

Motion-era tagline:

```text
From market signal to software proof.
```

## Visual Identity

### Palette

Use the media-kit colors as recognition assets and the site colors as production roles.

| Role | Color | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Deep ink | `#061114` | Primary dark background, motion canvas, nav-like fields |
| Panel ink | `#0D1D22` | Cards, proof panels, subtle content bands |
| Very dark blue | `#0E6B99` | Depth, section structure, secondary surfaces |
| Dark blue | `#1EA5B4` | Charts, proof paths, secondary highlights |
| Light blue | `#37FFDE` | Primary accent, active states, investor CTAs, signal pulses |
| White | `#D9D9D9` | Logo variants, body text on dark, external-use media assets |
| Gray | `#444444` | Dividers, restraint, secondary labels |

Guardrails:

- Do not make the system a one-note cyan interface.
- Keep neutral dark surfaces, sharp hierarchy, and restrained accent use.
- Use light blue for decision points, not for every decorative element.

### Typography

Primary production type:

- Use a clean sans-serif system for all readable copy. The site uses Inter/system sans.

Display asset:

- `"You're Gone"` belongs mainly to the logo, wordmark, and short static display moments.
- If Canva cannot use the font reliably, keep the wordmark as an imported logo asset and use a clean sans-serif for editable text.

Avoid:

- Long investor copy in `"You're Gone"`.
- Dense captions in decorative fonts.
- Tiny all-caps labels that lose legibility on mobile.

### Logo And Mark Behavior

The CipherPlay symbol is a forward play shape with circuit-like node arms. Treat it as a technical signal mark, not a mascot.

Approved uses:

- Symbol as a quiet corner mark.
- Wordmark as intro/outro lockup.
- Gradient symbol as a premium hero or social-card anchor.
- Background mark as a large, cropped, low-opacity trust cue.

Motion uses:

- Node pulses along the circuit arms.
- A short left-to-right or node-to-node trace before the full mark resolves.
- A 1-2 percent scale settle at the end of intros.

Avoid:

- Spinning the mark.
- 3D extrusion.
- Glitch effects that imply instability.
- Recoloring outside the approved blue/cyan/white family.

## Layout System

Use the live site as the reusable layout grammar.

### Core Structures

- Hero proof panel: message on one side, mark/proof links on the other.
- Three-proof ladder: `01 / Published Market Research`, `02 / Product Execution`, `03 / Partner Opportunity Formation`.
- Audience cards: Investors, Partners, Customers.
- Product spotlight: RANDAO as the current product proof anchor.
- Investor CTA panel: private materials request after public context.

### Shape Rules

- Cards: 8px radius, subtle cyan border, dark panel fill.
- Bands: full-width dark or near-dark sections, not floating decorative card stacks.
- Buttons: high-contrast cyan fill for investor CTAs, restrained text links for secondary paths.
- Proof chips: short, factual, and bounded.

### Information Density

CipherPlay should feel useful and diligence-ready. Favor:

- concise proof blocks;
- clear hierarchy;
- numbered evidence paths;
- audience-specific next steps;
- screenshots, charts, diagrams, and research artifacts when real.

Avoid:

- generic futuristic atmosphere;
- oversized abstract hero text without proof nearby;
- decorative gradients as the whole brand signal;
- over-explaining the studio model before giving the audience a route.

## Motion Language

Default motion personality:

```text
Precise, technical, evidence-aware, and calm.
```

Use motion to reveal structure and proof. Do not use motion to create hype.

### Timing

- Social card animation: 6-8 seconds.
- Reel/story short: 8-12 seconds.
- Carousel page animation: 3-5 seconds per page if exported as video.
- Intro logo resolve: 0.7-1.2 seconds.
- Card entry: 160-280 ms.
- Proof ladder sequence: 240-420 ms between steps.
- CTA settle: final 600-900 ms.

### Easing

- Use smooth ease-out for card entries.
- Use linear or subtle ease-in-out for background dot-field movement.
- Use crisp snap timing for proof cards, but avoid bouncy cartoon motion.

### Signature Motifs

1. Circuit trace
   - A cyan line or dot travels through the symbol arms, then the mark resolves.

2. Dot-field drift
   - Low-opacity ring/dot clusters drift slowly behind the content, echoing the media-kit backgrounds.

3. Proof ladder build
   - Numbered proof cards enter in sequence, giving investor diligence a visible path.

4. Research-to-product loop
   - Three stages reveal in order: Market Research, Product Thesis, Software Proof.

5. CTA lock-in
   - The final investor CTA gains a restrained cyan border/fill, then holds steady.

### Avoided Motions

- Glitch, static, or signal failure effects.
- Fast crypto-trading style flashes.
- Meme-like kinetic type.
- Spinning coins, token rain, neon tunnels, or generic blockchain grids.
- Excessive blur, bloom, or bokeh.

## Creative Routes

### Route A: Investor Diligence Signal

Best for:

- investor material prompts;
- fundraising-adjacent posts;
- public-company overview cards;
- founder updates about CipherPlay progress.

Formula:

1. Open with a bounded strategic claim.
2. Reveal the proof ladder.
3. Close with a public-context-first investor CTA.

Example locked copy:

```text
Research-backed software venture formation.
Market Research -> Product Thesis -> Software Proof.
Get investor materials after reviewing the public context.
```

### Route B: Research To Product

Best for:

- Market Research announcements;
- report launch cards;
- research insight clips;
- educational carousels.

Formula:

1. Lead with market uncertainty.
2. Show the research signal.
3. Connect the signal to a product thesis or technical path.
4. Keep Full Reports as the deeper request path.

Example locked copy:

```text
Noisy market. Clearer signal.
CipherPlay publishes Market Research to make emerging-technology opportunity easier to evaluate.
```

### Route C: RANDAO Product Proof

Best for:

- RANDAO demos;
- blockchain infrastructure proof posts;
- product spotlight cards;
- technical credibility moments.

Formula:

1. Name RANDAO as CipherPlay-owned and operated.
2. State the factual product category.
3. Tie it back to research-backed execution.

Example locked copy:

```text
RANDAO is CipherPlay-owned and operated.
Decentralized and verifiable randomness for blockchain and cryptographic infrastructure.
```

### Route D: Partner Ecosystem Leverage

Best for:

- partnership posts;
- event recaps;
- ecosystem collaboration;
- co-marketing prompts.

Formula:

1. Lead with mutual leverage.
2. Clarify the collaboration type.
3. Avoid inflated logo language.

Example locked copy:

```text
Research collaboration, product validation, ecosystem access.
Partner with a studio that can research, prototype, and ship.
```

## Canva Animation Template System

This is not a native Canva "animation kit" object. It is a reusable template system to build in Canva using the Brand Kit, uploaded assets, saved templates, and page/element animations.

### Canva Brand Kit Setup

Add:

- Colors: `#061114`, `#0D1D22`, `#0E6B99`, `#1EA5B4`, `#37FFDE`, `#D9D9D9`, `#444444`.
- Logos: `Logo_Gradient.svg`, `Logo.svg`, `LogoText_V1.svg`, `Logo_Solid_White.svg`.
- Backgrounds: `Background_Logo_2560x1440.jpg`, `Background_LogoTextV0_2560x1440.jpg`, `Banner_Website_1200x630.jpg`.
- Display font: `You're Gone.otf` if Canva account permissions support uploaded brand fonts.

### Template 1: Investor Proof Social Card

Format:

- 1080x1080 Instagram/LinkedIn square.
- 1200x630 link preview variant.

Editable fields:

- `headline`
- `proof_01`
- `proof_02`
- `proof_03`
- `cta_label`
- `source_note`

Motion:

- Dot-field background starts at 98 percent scale and slowly pans.
- Logo symbol fades in at top right.
- Headline enters from 12px upward with opacity.
- Proof cards enter `01`, `02`, `03` in sequence.
- CTA locks in during the last second.

### Template 2: Research-To-Product Reel

Format:

- 1080x1920 vertical video or story.
- 8-12 seconds.

Editable fields:

- `opening_market_signal`
- `research_point`
- `product_thesis`
- `software_proof`
- `cta_label`

Motion:

- Three stacked stages reveal as a vertical path.
- Light-blue trace line connects the stages.
- Final frame resolves to the wordmark and CTA.

### Template 3: RANDAO Product Proof

Format:

- 1080x1080 square.
- 1080x1920 story/reel.

Editable fields:

- `product_name`
- `product_category`
- `proof_statement`
- `demo_cta`

Motion:

- Circuit-node pulse opens the frame.
- RANDAO proof statement enters in a compact card.
- CipherPlay ownership line remains visible before the demo CTA.

### Template 4: Partner Ecosystem Tile

Format:

- 1080x1080 square.
- 1920x1080 presentation/video slide.

Editable fields:

- `partner_context`
- `collaboration_type`
- `mutual_value`
- `cta_label`

Motion:

- Logo/relationship entries fade in as labeled proof blocks.
- Keep relationship labels accurate: Backer, Partner, or Partner ecosystem.
- No endorsement-style motion or customer-wall treatment.

### Template 5: Report Launch Carousel

Format:

- 4-6 square carousel pages.

Pages:

1. Report title and market question.
2. Public signal.
3. Why it matters.
4. What CipherPlay is watching.
5. Full Report CTA.
6. Optional source/context page.

Motion:

- If exported as video, use page-level fade or slide, with per-page proof-card entry.
- Keep citations and source notes static and readable.

## Claim And Compliance Rules

Safe factual claims:

- CipherPlay is an emerging-technology software studio and market research firm.
- CipherPlay publishes Market Research.
- CipherPlay owns and operates RANDAO.
- RANDAO is a product for decentralized and verifiable randomness in blockchain and cryptographic infrastructure.
- TAP means Transparency, Authenticity, and Perspicacity.
- Listed backers and partner ecosystem entries may appear only with their approved relationship labels.

Use with care:

- Venture-backed as an investor-facing credibility modifier, not the default public category.
- Investable software opportunities as strategic framing, not a performance promise.
- Market Intelligence only as request-gated deeper research, not public Market Research.

Forbidden without owner review:

- Customer names.
- Revenue, retention, performance, or traction.
- Guaranteed outcomes.
- "Best", "leading", "fastest", "most advanced", or similar superiority claims.
- Partnership implications stronger than the documented relationship label.
- Investment advice or regulated financial claims.

## Review Checklist

Before publishing any social animation or Canva template:

- Copy matches `CONTEXT.md` public language.
- Any relationship label is accurate.
- RANDAO is clearly CipherPlay-owned and operated when used as proof.
- Investor path stays visible when the creative is master-brand work.
- CTA matches audience mode.
- Logo and wordmark are not distorted.
- Text remains readable on mobile.
- Motion is calm, precise, and proof-revealing.
- Generated imagery contains no readable fake text, claims, logos, charts, or UI.
- The asset does not make CipherPlay feel like a generic agency, crypto hype brand, personal brand, or disconnected product portfolio.
