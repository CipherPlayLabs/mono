CipherPlay's `/info/` content site establishes a public home for company information,
Market Research reports, product pages, partner pages, team details, and approved
brand assets. The site gives investors, partners, and customers a clear way to review
CipherPlay's public company, research, product, and ecosystem context.

## What's Here

### Site Sections
Dedicated pages for company information, Market Research, products, partners, and media
assets help readers reach the relevant product, research, team, and partner context
quickly. Investor materials stay request-gated through hosted form flows rather than a
public page for private investor material.

### Market Research Reports
State Of Web3 gives readers public Market Research they can inspect first, while
Full Report access keeps the deeper evidence pack request-gated.

### Products & Services
RANDAO is the public blockchain infrastructure product page and points to the live
product and whitepaper. The products page also introduces current consulting services
and clearly marked historic sunset projects.

### Media Kit
Approved CipherPlay logos, banners, colors, and display font assets are available for
preview and download. The media kit supports external materials; it is not the source
for public traction, endorsement, or private investor claims.

## Technical Details

The site is built with:

- **Docusaurus v3** with TypeScript
- **Company, partner, and product pages** backed by typed site data
- **Same-origin analytics** via private operational Plausible infrastructure

## What's Next

Conversion CTAs now point to n8n-hosted request flows for investor materials,
partnerships, consulting discovery, and Full Report access.
