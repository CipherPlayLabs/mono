CipherPlay's `/info/` content site establishes a public home for company context,
audience-specific entry points, market analysis teasers, product proof, team details,
and approved brand assets.

## What's Here

### Audience Routes
Dedicated pages for investors, analysts, partners, and customers help each audience
reach the most relevant product, research, team, and industry context quickly.

### Market Analysis
Public teaser pages summarize CipherPlay's market framing without publishing full
private reports.

### Products
Randao is the first public product page and points to the live product and whitepaper.

### Media Kit
Approved CipherPlay logos and banners are available for preview and download.

## Technical Details

The site is built with:

- **Docusaurus v3** with TypeScript
- **Audience and product pages** backed by typed site data
- **Local search** via `@easyops-cn/docusaurus-search-local`
- **Same-origin analytics** via private operational Plausible infrastructure

## RSS Feeds

Newsroom and archived content feeds remain available:

- [Newsroom RSS](https://allanbpediniv.com/info/newsroom/rss.xml)
- [Archived Research RSS](https://allanbpediniv.com/info/research/rss.xml)
- [Archived Insights RSS](https://allanbpediniv.com/info/insights/rss.xml)

## What's Next

Conversion forms and deeper report access will be enabled after the external request
URLs are approved.
