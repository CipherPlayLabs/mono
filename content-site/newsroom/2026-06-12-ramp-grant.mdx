CipherPlay has received a $10,000 grant from [RAMP](https://ramprb.com/), the
Regional Accelerator and Mentoring Program. The grant adds regional startup ecosystem
support as CipherPlay continues building public Market Research, emerging-technology
product theses, and software execution capacity.

RAMP helps technology-based businesses connect with entrepreneurial resources,
mentorship, and regional support. For CipherPlay, that kind of backing is especially
relevant because the company sits between market learning and product execution: it
studies emerging technology markets, forms product theses, and then turns the strongest
signals into software, services, and partner opportunities.

## Why RAMP Matters

The grant gives CipherPlay another concrete link to Virginia's builder ecosystem. RAMP
is focused on helping technology companies move through the hard early stages of
company development: customer discovery, market analysis, mentorship, investor
readiness, and startup execution.

That focus matches CipherPlay's own operating model. CipherPlay's public site is built
so customers, partners, investors, and ecosystem groups can inspect the company's
research, products, team, backers, and partner relationships before a private
conversation begins.

## What The Grant Supports

The $10,000 grant helps CipherPlay continue investing in the work that makes the
company legible and useful to the market:

- Market Research that makes emerging-technology opportunities easier to understand;
- product development around RANDAO, CipherPlay's product brand for decentralized and
  verifiable randomness;
- infrastructure and software consulting for teams that need research-backed planning,
  prototypes, integrations, and implementation support;
- partner ecosystem development across accelerators, technical communities, companies,
  and venture-support networks.

The funding also strengthens CipherPlay's backer record. In the company's public
glossary, a backer is an organization that has provided financial support. Naming that
support publicly helps keep CipherPlay's credibility signals checkable rather than
hidden behind private decks or vague traction claims.
